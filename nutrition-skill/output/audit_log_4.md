## Iron Absorption Optimization Summary

This plan was specifically designed to maximize dietary iron absorption for a client with clinically significant iron deficiency (ferritin 8 ng/mL):

### Strategies implemented:
1. **Heme iron sources daily:** Red meat (beef, lamb, duck) 3-4x/week; fish/seafood (salmon, tuna, sardines, mussels, shrimp) 3-4x/week; dark meat poultry (thighs) as alternative
2. **Non-heme iron sources daily:** Lentils, chickpeas, black beans, kidney beans, edamame, quinoa, buckwheat, dark chocolate, pumpkin seeds, sunflower seeds, sesame, tahini, spinach, kale
3. **Vitamin C pairing at EVERY iron meal:** Orange/grapefruit juice at breakfast; bell peppers, tomatoes, lemon juice at lunch/dinner; kiwi, strawberries, citrus at snacks
4. **Calcium separation:** Near-zero dairy in plan; when present (1 instance of tvorog), minimum 2h separation; protein shakes ALWAYS on water, never milk
5. **Tannin avoidance:** No coffee or tea included in any meal
6. **Supplements:** Prescribed iron supplement (doctor-dosed) + Vitamin C 500mg with iron meals + whey isolate on water

### Foods explicitly excluded (client preference):
- Liver, kidney, and all organ meats -- compensated with doubled legume and seed intake
